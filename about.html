About Us

Trusted guides on difficult terrain.

We are executive coaches, facilitators, and justice seekers who work with leaders and teams in the social impact space.

We've partnered since 2018, after meeting at the Teleos Coaching Development Program.

Our work is rooted in the shared values of love and empathy, and in the powerful change that can happen when we are in deeply resonant relationships. As white, cis, straight women, we commit to maintaining awareness of our privileged identities and to centering anti-racism and liberatory consciousness in our work.

We work with movement, government, and non-profit leaders instead of corporate clients, because we understand their unique challenges — and because we're passionate about doing work we love alongside people we respect.

Our Process

We listen first. We name what feels present but unspoken.

We function as trusted guides on difficult terrain, bringing clarity and compassion — holding strategy and systems alongside emotion, identity, and lived experience. We invite pause, not as an escape, but as a way back to insight, perspective, and wiser action.

Our coaching and facilitation are grounded, human, and attuned to all of what leadership asks of people now. We center joy, humanity, and connection — and we don't turn away from tension or conflict when it shows up.
