Coaching to Support Leadership Development

1:1 coaching for an expanding team.

The Partnership

Empower Project, a national leader in relational organizing, began partnering with Work Better Together in early 2024. The organization offered opt-in 1:1 coaching to its expanding leadership and management team. As of early 2026, the partnership continues with ongoing 1:1 coaching, 2:2 founder coaching, and team facilitation. New leaders learn about the coaching approach at onboarding and choose whether to participate.

The Initial Program

Over the six months preceding the 2024 elections, we supported Empower's rapidly growing leadership team. Executive Director Amanda Brink expanded coaching access to additional staff in the final weeks before the election. By November 4th, we had engaged with 19 team members through individual and group coaching adapted to their work pressures. Some leaders met weekly; others used different cadences. Several completed Prism Personality Assessments to inform coaching, and 90-minute group sessions were offered near election day, when stress peaked.

The Challenge

Empower's leadership faced intense demands and emotional strain from managing quickly-built teams designed only for the campaign cycle. Amanda sought coaching to help leaders manage stress, improve communication, and maintain mission focus.

The Transformation

Coaching emphasized emotional intelligence, strategic thinking, and habits supporting effective team dynamics. Participants reported significant personal and professional growth, directly attributing leadership improvements to coaching.

I think I have become more emotionally intelligent and aware of how I show up in the workplace.Empower team member
I always took a very left-brained approach before, and now I'm better at thinking about how my team is feeling and letting that guide what they might need from me as a leader.Empower team member

Continuous Support Through 2026

The partnership deepened as Empower evolved. Sustained 1:1 coaching, 2:2 founder coaching, and sub-team facilitation support the organization holistically. Sustained relationships at multiple levels let us surface insights and grounded observations while maintaining confidentiality — strengthening organizational learning and adaptive capacity.

Why It Matters

Empower's collaboration demonstrates the value of sustained, multi-level leadership support. Simultaneous individual coaching, founder partnership, and team facilitation strengthen both personal capacity and organizational connective tissue — enabling leaders to recognize patterns, anticipate challenges, and make aligned real-time decisions. The result is improved individual performance and a more self-aware, adaptive leadership culture equipped to navigate complexity.
