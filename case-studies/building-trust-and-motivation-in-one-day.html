The Partnership

In spring 2025, Work Better Together facilitated a retreat for Planned Parenthood Federation of America's Policy Team, addressing trust-building, expectation clarity, and leadership transitions.

The Challenge

A team leader explained the need for outside support:

I couldn't fully participate when I tried facilitating internally. We needed outside support for real participation, and to work through deep challenges.

The leadership team engaged external facilitation to provide structure, emotional intelligence, and grounded support around motivation, recognition, and alignment.

Our Approach

Using a custom-designed retreat emphasizing present-moment awareness, we helped the team tackle three key challenges.

Motivation awareness

Team members completed Prism personality assessments with individual coaching sessions — discovering that motivation is individual, and that assumptions about others' drivers often miss the mark.

Recognition that lands

The group explored how personal styles shape what appreciation means, from paired shares to values-based feedback.

Shared values, clear agreements

Together, the team defined how timeliness, quality, and collaboration function in practice — creating shared accountability language.
